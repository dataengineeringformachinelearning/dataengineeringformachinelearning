App Purpose & Data Transparency

The Data Engineering for Machine Learning Platform is designed to ingestion-test, monitor, and analyze system-wide telemetry. To facilitate authentic and secure administration and ingest real-time analytics, our platform offers integrations with services such as Google Analytics 4 (GA4).

We request user authentication and data access strictly for the following purposes:

  • check_circle_outline System Telemetry Analysis: Syncing traffic parameters to train PyTorch neural network anomaly models (ThreatModel).
  • check_circle_outline Manager Authentication: Securely authenticating system administrators for pipeline management actions.
  • check_circle_outline SLA & Performance Optimization: Identifying geographic latency bottlenecks and ensuring high-availability targets are met.

Your privacy is our priority. We host our app on our own verified domains and do not share your private account data with third parties.
