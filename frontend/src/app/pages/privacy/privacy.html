1. Introduction

Data Engineering for Machine Learning ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, read our platform documentation, or interact with our system status services.

3. GDPR Compliance (EU User Rights)

If you are located in the European Union (EU) or European Economic Area (EEA), you are granted specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). These include:

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal data when no longer needed.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Request that we limit how we process your information.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transmission of your data to another organization in a structured format.

GDPR Data Request Center

Use this form to request an export or permanent deletion of any personal data collected during your sessions.

4. CCPA/CPRA Compliance (California Rights)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have specific rights regarding their personal information:

  • Right to Know: Discover what personal information we collect, source, and share.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Direct us to stop selling or sharing your personal information (Note: We do not sell or share any user personal information with third parties).
  • Right to Limit: Restrict the use of sensitive personal information.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Enjoy equal service and pricing even if you exercise your privacy rights.

5. Security & Data Retention

We utilize industry-standard security protocols, including HTTPS/TLS encryption for all api communication, secure cookie parameters, and role-based database constraints. We retain your telemetry logs and system statuses only as long as necessary to fulfill the services outlined on this platform.
